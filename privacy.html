Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to La Prime Caffe ("we," "our," or "us"). We represent our restaurant located in Los Angeles, CA. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at the address provided below.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products.

  • Personal Information: We do not collect credit card information directly on this website.
  • Automatically Collected Data: When you visit our website, our servers may automatically log standard data provided by your web browser, such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to handle specific functions of our business. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

  • Uber Eats: We use Uber Eats for delivery and order processing. When you place an order, your transaction is processed entirely through the Uber Eats platform. We do not store your payment details on our servers.
  • Google Maps & Apple Maps: We use these services to provide location and direction information. These services may collect data about your location tailored to their own privacy practices.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon processes (via third-party apps).
  • To send you marketing and promotional communications (only if you opt-in).
  • To improve our website and customer experience.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to access or store information. You can refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser.

6. Your Privacy Rights (California)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information, including the right to request access to your data and the right to request deletion of your data.
